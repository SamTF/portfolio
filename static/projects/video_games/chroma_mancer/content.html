Summary

A top down bullet hell game with RPG elements where you harness the power of Colours and create your own character! The drawing determines your abilities, as each Colour is a gameplay stat and grants you specific bonuses.


Will you strive to max out your stats or to create an artistic masterpiece as you fight the horde of monsters and collect all colours?
